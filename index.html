Why I Code

I went into Web Development for the constant growth of the industry. There are always new technologies to learn, and I want to learn them all.

Coming from a background of customer service, I constantly worked with interfaces that were counter-intuitive, frustrating, and outdated. I've always had a passion for code and experimented on the side, so it got me thinking that things didn't need to be the way they were. This led me to thinking about what I could do next and how I could make an impact on the industry.

As a developer I've worked with Node.js, Angular, Express, HTML, CSS, MongoDB and experimented with WebRTC among other things. I'm ready to take on any challenge that comes my way and work for a company who's dream I believe in.
